About me

Since I was a child I really liked to devise and build simple things, this led me to what I am today. I am passionate about robotics and AI solutions that help people in their daily lives.

My mentality at work is to be persevering, creative, and to learn something new every day. I am currently working at Kalman Robotics developing educational robotics content and an autonomous mobile robot kit based on ROS2 and micro-ROS. I thrive in environments where innovation and hands-on problem solving are at the core.

I am proud to have published at three international conferences: HRI 2022 (ACM/IEEE, Japan), C3 2025 (IEEE, Colombia), and UR 2026 (Osaka, Japan). I was also a finalist at the ICSR 2021 social robotics competition and won the PIIB 2022 biomedical engineering award.

Looking ahead, I aspire to pursue a master's degree in Robotics and AI to deepen my expertise in autonomous navigation, manipulation, and intelligent systems. My goal is to develop robotic solutions with real social impact.
